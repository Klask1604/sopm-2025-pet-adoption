About PawsHome

Our mission to connect pets with loving families

Our Story

Founded in 2020, PawsHome began with a simple vision: to ensure that every pet finds a loving, forever home. What started as a small volunteer operation in Brasov has grown into one of Romania's most trusted pet adoption organizations.

We believe that every animal deserves a second chance at happiness. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome abandoned and surrendered pets. We provide medical care, behavioral training, and lots of love to prepare our animals for their new families.

Over the years, we've successfully placed over 2,000 pets in loving homes, and we're just getting started. Every adoption is a celebration, and every pet that finds their forever family reminds us why we do what we do.
